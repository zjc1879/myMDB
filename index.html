Rice

Rice is a favorite staple food in China, even in East and Southeast Asia. It is steamed or stewed with rice and proper amount of water.

Nutrition

A portion of white rice contains about 65% water, 25 ~ 27% starch, 2.5 ~ 3% protein, and 0.3 ~ 0.5% fat. At the same time, it contains many other micronutrients, but the content is small. Brown rice is not to mention, in addition to starch, its fat, trace elements, dietary fiber and other nutrients content is higher, nutrition is also more abundant.

Type

Rice is divided into indica rice, japonica rice and glutinous rice. 1. Indica rice is made of indica non glutinous rice, and its grains are generally long oval or slender. According to the harvest season of indica rice, it can be divided into early indica rice and late indica rice. 2. Japonica rice is made of Japonica non glutinous rice, and its grains are generally oval. According to the harvest season of Japonica rice, it can be divided into Early Japonica Rice and late japonica rice. 3. Glutinous rice is made of glutinous rice. It is milky white, opaque, translucent and sticky. It can be divided into two types: indica glutinous rice and japonica glutinous rice. Indica glutinous rice is made of indica glutinous rice, and its grains are generally long oval or slender.
